South Carolina Auto Dealer Bond

The South Carolina Department of Motor Vehicles requires wholesalers post a surety bond in the amount of $15,000 ONLY if they plan to wholesale at auto auctions, motorcycle dealers, or motorcycle wholesalers.

A wholesale dealer who desires to offer, attempt to sell, or exhibit a motor vehicle in any form or manner other than the listed entities must obtain a retail dealer license.
